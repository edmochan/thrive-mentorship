# Sample Meeting Agendas and Templates
## Quick Agenda Template

- Check-in (5-10 min)
- Focus topic (20-30 min)
- Questions & discussion (10-15 min)
- Action items & next steps (5 min)

---

## 1\. First Meeting (Relationship Building & Goal Setting)

**Objectives:**

- Build rapport
- Establish expectations
- Define goals

**Agenda:**

- Introductions
  - Background, interests, career journey (mentor)
  - Academic path, interests, aspirations (mentee)
- Goal Setting
  - Discuss mentee goals
  - Identify priorities
- Logistics
  - Confirm meeting schedule
  - Communication preferences

**Possible action Items:**

- Mentee drafts/refines goals
- Mentor provides initial resources

---

## 2\. Early Meetings (Career Exploration)

**Objectives:**

- Build awareness of industry norms
- Explore career pathways

**Agenda:**

- Check-in
  - Wins, challenges, updates
- Topic Discussion
  - Career paths in the field
  - Workplace expectations (communication, teamwork, etc)
- Q&A and Action Items

**Possible action Items:**

- Informational interview, resume draft, or reflection task

---

## 3\. Midpoint Meeting (Progress & Skill Development)

**Objectives:**

- Assess progress toward goals
- Develop concrete skills

**Agenda:**

- Check-in
- Progress Review
  - What's working?
  - What needs adjustment?
- Skill Focus
  - Resume/portfolio review
  - Mock interview
  - Communication practice
- Feedback Exchange
  - Mentor → mentee
  - Mentee → mentor (what support is helpful)

**Possible action Items:**

- Revise materials, apply to opportunities, practice skills

---

## 4\. Advanced Meetings (Career Preparation & Application)

**Objectives:**

- Apply learning to real opportunities
- Build confidence

**Agenda:**

- Check-in (applications, interviews, coursework)
- Deep Dive Topic
  - Interview strategies
  - Navigating internship environments
  - Networking strategies
- Scenario Practice
  - Behavioral questions
  - Workplace scenarios
- Mentor Insights
  - Personal experiences, lessons learned

**Possible action Items:**

- Submit applications, schedule mock interviews, expand network

---

## 5\. Final Meeting (Reflection & Next Steps)

**Objectives:**

- Reflect on growth
- Plan next steps

**Agenda:**

- Check-in (applications, interviews, coursework)
- Progress Review
  - Revisit initial goals
  - Identify accomplishments
- Future Planning
  - Next steps (internships, applications, skills)
  - Continued mentorship or networking

**Possible action Items:**

- Finalize career plan
- Connect on LinkedIn or maintain contact plan
