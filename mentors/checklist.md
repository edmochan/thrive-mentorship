# Meeting Preparation and Reflection Checklists

## Before Each Meeting (5 minutes prep)

☐ Review mentee's goals and last meeting notes

☐ Identify 1 focus area (skill, competency, or topic)

☐ Prepare 1-2 guiding questions

☐ Consider a relevant example or story from your experience

---

## During the Meeting

### 1\. Build Connection

☐ Start with a check-in (academic, personal, career updates)

☐ Create a welcoming, non-judgmental space

☐ Acknowledge effort and small wins

### 2\. Guide the Conversation

☐ Ask open-ended questions

☐ Encourage the mentee to reflect and lead

☐ Listen actively (don't jump to solutions too quickly)

### 3\. Make the Hidden Curriculum Explicit

☐ Explain _how_ things work in practice (not just what to do)

☐ Clarify expectations in professional settings

☐ Share common mistakes and how to avoid them

### 4\. Provide Feedback Effectively

☐ Start with strengths

☐ Be specific and actionable

☐ Offer examples or alternatives

☐ Check that feedback is understood

### 6\. Encourage Growth Mindset

☐ Normalize uncertainty and mistakes

☐ Reinforce that learning is a process

☐ Reframe challenges as opportunities

### 7\. Close with Clear Next Steps

☐ Agree on 1-2 action items

☐ Ensure goals are specific and achievable

☐ Confirm next meeting time

---

## After the Meeting: Reflection

☐ Did the mentee leave with clarity?

☐ Did I make at least one "unwritten rule" visible?

☐ Did I support both skill-building _and_ confidence?

### Common Pitfalls

☐ Talking more than listening

☐ Assuming prior knowledge

☐ Giving vague advice ("just network")

☐ Overloading with too much information

☐ Focusing only on technical skills (ignore holistic growth)

### High-Impact Mentoring Includes

☐ Personal stories (especially mistakes or learning moments)

☐ Describing real workplace scenarios

☐ Validating their experiences and concerns

☐ Actively building their confidence
