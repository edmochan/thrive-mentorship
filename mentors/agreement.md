## 5\. Roles and Responsibilities

**Mentor Responsibilities**

- Provide guidance on career pathways and industry expectations
- Offer constructive, actionable feedback
- Be respectful, inclusive, and responsive
- Maintain appropriate professional boundaries and confidentiality
- Provide the mentee with the opportunity to visit the Google site, if possible
- Alert your on-campus program coordinator if any concerns are raised about the mentee
- Complete a survey at the end of the year reflecting on the experience

**Mentee Responsibilities**

- Come prepared with questions or updates
- Follow through on agreed-upon action items
- Communicate clearly and respectfully
- Be open to feedback and new perspectives
- Take ownership of personal and professional growth
- Alert your on-campus program coordinator if any concerns are raised about the mentor
- Complete a survey at the end of the year reflecting on the experience
