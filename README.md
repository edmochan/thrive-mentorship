# THRIVE: Toolkit of Holistic Resources for Intentional and Versatile Engagement
## A Holistic Industry Mentoring Toolkit for Retention and Persistence of Undergraduate CS Students

This toolkit supports undergraduate computer science students and their 
industry mentors in building productive, meaningful mentoring relationships. 
It was developed at Carlow University through four years of program 
implementation and research.

### Who is this toolkit for?
- **Students** navigating their first professional relationships in tech
- **Industry mentors** who want to share their knowledge effectively
- **Program coordinators** seeking a replicable, low-cost mentoring model

### How to use this site
- [For Students](students/) — resources to help you make the most of mentoring
- [For Mentors](mentors/) — guides to help you share what you know
- [About the Research](research/) — theoretical framework and publications
- [Download Resources](resources/) — all templates and guides in one place
